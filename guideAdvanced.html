Advanced Guides

Advanced Shots

Advanced players should have all shots in their aresnal already. To further improve their game they should be working on the consistency of placement and power of all hits. When hitting a birdie, the shot should always be either tight to the net, have good placement, and make it as difficult for the opponent to hit as possible.

Advanced Footwork

Again advanced players should have all footwork techniques learned. To further their game it is simply a matter of improving these skills, both in execution and power. This would allow for much better shots, as well as faster movement around the court saving shots that would otherwise be lost.
