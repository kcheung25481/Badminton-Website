Beginner Guides

Beginner Shots

Most beginners start off with the lift and clear, as these are the most forgiving shots. As these simply require power, beginners can practice generating power in their wrist without worrying too much about placement of the birdie.

Beginner Footwork

Foot work is quite complicated in badminton, as some of the movements are counter-intuitive. For beginners, the easiest thing to work on is lunging with the same foot as your dominant hand. This is the most optimal way to reach for front court drops, as this allows you to turn your body to reach the farthest distnace to catch the shot.
