Intermediate Guides

Intermediate Shots

Next, intermediate players can work on more complicated shots. Drop shots are a powerful tool to add to a badminton player's arsenal, as it allows for mind games with the opponent. In a similar vein it is also good to learn smashes to kill the bird and win rallies.

Intermediate Footwork

Good footwork is what seperates badminton players from each other. By being able to move efficiently and effectively around the court is what allows for better shots and won games. Intermediate players should begin to add backwards, forwards, and sideway skips which allows for fast and explosive movement around the court.
